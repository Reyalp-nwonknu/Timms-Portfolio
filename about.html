About.

Hi, my name is Timm. Thanks for visiting my little portfolio website. I’m a mechanical engineer who has specialised on user centered product design and human-machine-interaction. Recently I’ve broadened my skillset by completing a course in User Experience Design. Some of the results you can review on this website. I was always convinced that creating new products is only useful when they make people’s lives a bit better. Now, as a UX designer, I learned how to understand the goals and needs of potential users even better and integrate them in a step-by-step approach to create new products. Apart from creating products for humans, I enjoy listening, playing and dancing to music, being outdoors and keeping myself fit with workout and (tower)running. If you want to learn more about me, feel free to contact me anytime!
